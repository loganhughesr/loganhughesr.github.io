These are notable projects of mine that don't necessarily fit on other parts of my website. Fifthwall is a Unity Package. Grain Room and Caterpillar are both musical instruments.

## FifthWall

An overview of the dynamic camera system for musical VR performances. Made with direction from Professor Anil Camci from the University of Michigan. The repository is currently private, but it will be released sometime soon.
